How to Compress Images Online

Our free image compressor allows you to reduce the file size of your images while maintaining visual quality. Simply upload your images, adjust the compression level, and download the optimized versions. This tool is perfect for:

  • Website optimization for faster loading
  • Reducing email attachment sizes
  • Preparing images for social media
  • Saving storage space on your devices

Why Compress Your Images?

Smaller image files load faster, use less bandwidth, and improve your website's SEO ranking. Google considers page speed as a ranking factor, so optimized images can help your site appear higher in search results.

Supported Image Formats

We support all major image formats including JPEG, PNG, and WebP. You can keep the original format or convert to a different format during compression.
